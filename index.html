Plastic

Plastics (or plastics) are materials from artificial or natural high molecular compounds (polymers). Synthetic polymers are the most common. People are advised to collect all species together clean and dry. At special points, they are separated by markings and sent to waste processing plants. Some species are not recycled and burned or buried in landfills.

Paper

Waste paper is the manufacturing waste of all types of paper and paperboard that can be reused as fibre. It includes newspapers, notebooks, magazines, cardboard boxes, flyers and brochures.In order to be properly accepted into the sorting center, you need to collect all the waste, separate the paper and cardboard. Then separate black and white, colored and illiquid waste. Further decompose by species (books, notebooks, magazines, documents, boxes, etc.), clean from foreign objects and materials, bind with ropes and pack. Store until delivery in a dry, ventilated room separate from other waste.

Glass

Glass recycling and glass scrap recycling includes such actions: collection of glass waste-. transportation to the recycling plant-. waste treatment consisting of several stages of separation, cleaning and washing-. Re-treatment, including crushing the glass hole, grinding into the smallest pieces, packing the resulting glass powder for further use.
